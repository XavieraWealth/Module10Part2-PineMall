ABOUT PINE CITY MALL

Welcome to Pine City Mall, the premier shopping destination in the heart of the city! Since opening in 2020, we've been committed to providing an exceptional experience for all our visitors.

Our three-level complex features over 100 stores including international brands, local boutiques, a state-of-the-art cinema complex, and diverse dining options from fast food to fine dining.

With ample parking, family-friendly facilities, and regular events, Pine City Mall is more than just a shopping center - it's a community hub where memories are made.
